Ramen in a Pot

The best (or easiest to make) source of nutritional value for your average college student!

Ingredients/Required Items

  • Pack of Ramen
  • Pot
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill the pot with water up to 1/3 of the way. You may need more water if using more than one pack.
  2. Place the water on the stove and allow to boil.
  3. Remove the noodles from its pack and place into the boiling water.
  4. As the noodles soften, pour the flavor packet into the pot.
  5. Using (preferably) a fork, separate the noodles from its blocky form. It is recommended to not use metallic objects, as they heat up in the boiling water.
  6. Pour the contents of the pot into a bowl. If you would like, you could drain the water into a strainer and place the noodles onto a plate.
